An Alchemical Standing

A Somatic Journey Through Three Realms

This is not an exercise; it is an act of listening. Your body is a vessel, a resonant chamber holding the echoes of the Earth below, the Cosmos above, and the haunted, bustling world of the Human in between. For the next three minutes, you will become a bridge. You will stand at the threshold of three realms and allow their whispers to move through your flesh. Do not seek answers; simply notice the sensations.

Preparation

Find a quiet space where you will not be disturbed. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, knees soft, spine aligned but not rigid. Close your eyes or soften your gaze. Take one deep breath and release it with a sigh, letting the mask of the day fall away.

I. The Realm of Earth
(The Root)

The Posture: Let your arms hang heavy by your sides. Gently turn your palms to face behind you. Let your arms drift a few inches away from your body with a subtle bend in your elbows. Feel as though your fingertips are heavy, dripping with ink, pulling towards the dark soil beneath the floor.

Somatic Inquiry

  • What is the texture of the ground felt through the soles of your feet?
  • What is the weight of your own history, felt in the marrow of your leg bones?
  • If a tremor were to rise from the Earth's core, where in your body would you feel it first?
  • What forgotten things are buried here in the dark soil of your being?

II. The Realm of Man
(The Heart)

The Posture: Slowly, as if moving through deep water, raise your arms in front of you to chest height. Your elbows should be bent and lower than your hands, your palms facing each other as if you are holding a large, invisible, fragile sphere. You are embracing a phantom.

Somatic Inquiry

  • What is the temperature in the sphere between your palms? A warmth, a chill, a static crackle?
  • Feel your pulse. Is it a steady drum, a frantic messenger, or a faint echo?
  • What unconscious shapes do your hands want to make around this sphere?
  • What faces, relationships, or archetypes flicker in the space you hold?

III. The Realm of Heaven
(The Crown)

The Posture: Release the sphere. Let your arms float upwards, reaching towards the sky above your head. As they reach their peak, rise onto the balls of your feet, balancing on the precipice. Stretch your fingers as if to touch a cold, distant star. You are an antenna for the void.

Somatic Inquiry

  • What is the pressure of the empty space above your skull? Vast, heavy, or liberating?
  • Notice the subtle tremble in your muscles. What is the frequency of this vibration?
  • If you could pull a silent piece of knowledge from the collective unconscious, how would it feel?
  • What color is the silence of the cosmos? Can you sense it behind your eyes?

Integration: The Return

Slowly, lower your heels back to the floor. Let your arms float down through the air, returning to your sides. Stand in stillness for a final moment. You have journeyed through three worlds and returned to yourself.

Which realm felt most familiar? Which felt most alien? What single sensation, image, or word has followed you back from your journey? This is your artifact from the descent. Hold it. It has something to tell you.
