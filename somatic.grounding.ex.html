Level 1: The Practice

Part I: The Foundation
(Establishing Structure)

The Posture (1 Minute): Stand with your feet grounded, arms resting by your sides. This first posture is the foundational structure from which all creativity can safely emerge. Connect with the physical support of the ground beneath you.

  • Notice the sensation of your feet on the floor. What information does this stable contact provide?
  • Feel the gentle pull of gravity. Can you allow your body to be supported by your skeleton without unnecessary tension?
  • What does it feel like to simply *be* in your body, here and now, within this foundational shape?

Part II: The Center
(Holding Creativity)

The Posture (1 Minute): Gently bring your hands forward to the level of your heart, palms facing each other as if holding a sphere. This is the center of your being, where you hold and nurture your own creative potential and felt sense.

  • Bring awareness to the space between your hands. Notice any sensations of warmth, energy, or stillness.
  • What is the rhythm of your breath as you hold this posture? Let it be natural, without needing to control it.
  • What is the quality of your inner experience right now? Acknowledge any feelings as part of your creative process.

Part III: The Expression
(Creative Action)

The Posture (1 Minute): From your center, extend your arms upward and rise gently onto the balls of your feet. This posture is an act of creative expression, of reaching from your grounded structure out into the world.

  • As you reach upwards, where do you feel a sense of opening or expansion in your body?
  • Notice the active engagement required to balance. How can you find both effort and ease in this expressive action?
  • What does it feel like to take up space, extending your energy from your core into the world around you?
