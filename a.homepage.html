Our Philosophy

Many of us are taught to chase a "perfect" or "Just Right" feeling. But for those with trauma or neurodivergence, this chase is a trap. It's a core feature of OCD known as the "Not Just Right Experience" (NJRE)â€”a constant, nagging sense that things are wrong.

We reclaim this. We believe the "not just right" feeling isn't a flaw; it's a feature. It's a signal from a system that has creatively adapted to survive. Instead of fighting for an impossible perfection, we find the kindness and beauty in the imperfect.

Embrace the 'Not Just Right'

Your authentic self isn't flawless. It's resilient. It's real. That's where kindness begins.
