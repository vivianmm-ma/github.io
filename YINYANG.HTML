The CindnessKult Philosophy

Character Sheet: Debuffs & Conditions

A dysregulated system creates real-world consequences. Low vagal tone is a primary driver of chronic inflammation, the root of many difficult boss battles.

  • Autoimmune Disease: Friendly fire is enabled. Your defense system attacks your own character.
  • POTS & Dysautonomia: Your controls are inverted. Basic functions like heart rate become unpredictable.
  • hEDS & MCAS: Your character model has collision issues. Trauma exacerbates physical instability.
  • OCD & Anxiety: Your character gets stuck in behavioral loops to cope with overwhelming internal data.
